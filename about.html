Walk with us

We are England's largest network of happy walk schemes, helping thousands of people across the country to enjoy a more active lifestyle.

Our group walks are led by friendly, specially trained volunteers who are on hand to provide encouragement and support, and make sure no one gets left behind.

Our walks are short and over easy terrain. They are open to everyone, but are especially aimed at those who are least active.

Below is a list of cities where you can find us
  • London
  • Manchester
  • Liverpool
  • Bath
  • Bristol

For more information please contact us.
